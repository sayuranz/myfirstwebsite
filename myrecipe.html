My Favourite Recipe: Lamb Fried Maggi

Ingredients (for 1 serving) :

  • Instant Noodles (maggi)
  • Fresh lamb
  • Mixed veggies
  • Curry spice
  • A pinch of MSG
  • 2 diced garlic
  • 1/2 onion diced
  • 1 egg

Instructions :

Noodles

  1. boil the noodles with high heat in a pot for 3 minutes
  2. once the noodles fully cooked strain the noodles with a colander
  3. pour a little bit of cooking oil in a pan with a medium heat
  4. once it heated cook the diced onions and garlic
  5. crack an egg and make it scrambled
  6. pour a lil bit of water with the instant noodles seasoning
  7. once it all cooked, start frying the noodles

Lamb fried
